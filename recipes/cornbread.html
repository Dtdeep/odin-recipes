Grandma's Cornbread Dressing

Description

Cornbread dressing is a classic Southern side dish made from crumbled cornbread mixed with sautéed onions, celery, herbs, and flavorful broth, then baked until moist on the inside and lightly crisp on top. Often served during holiday meals like Thanksgiving, it has a savory, comforting taste with a slightly sweet, rustic corn flavor. It’s hearty, aromatic, and pairs beautifully with roasted meats and gravy.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups crumbled cornbread
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 tablespoon dried sage, or to taste
